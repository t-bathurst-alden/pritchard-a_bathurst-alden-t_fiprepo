Intro Paragraph

Zima was a brand initially started in 1993, a drink that was quickly forgotten in the eyes of the public due to criticisms about being “boring” and “unmanly”, but in recent times, Zima has gotten the rebrand it needed!
Zima had tried in the past to revive itself, but only for limited time releases and minor brand deals in Japan.
Now, Zima is a resurrected drink, with a fresh new look and a fresh new formula, no longer associated with one specific gender or crowd but a fun wine cooler for all!
