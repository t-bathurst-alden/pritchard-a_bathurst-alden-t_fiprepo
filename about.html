Intro To Zima

Zima was a brand initially started in 1993, a drink that was quickly forgotten in the eyes of the public due to criticisms about being “boring” and “unmanly”, but in recent times, Zima has gotten the rebrand it needed!

Zima had tried in the past to revive itself, but only for limited time releases and minor brand deals in Japan.

Zima is a resurrected drink, with a fresh new look and a fresh new formula, no longer associated with one specific gender or crowd but a fun wine cooler for all!

In the new generation of Zima, our wonderful company has decided to make the irresistible flavours of Zima taste even more fresh, made our branding even more eye-catching, and expanded our target demographic. This new era of Zima hopes to usher in a new wave of popularity for the brand, so that our dear customers can enjoy Zima for years to come.

Changes Zima Plans To Implement

As a part of Zima's new face, we have also changed the way we make our alcohol. We've upgraded from 5% to 7% alcohol content in each bottle or can of Zima, resulting in a delicious and strong beverage to mix in with other drinks or enjoy on your own.

The citrus theme of Zima is our staple, and we are committed to coming up with fun, fresh new flavours as our brand grows. For more news about Zima products coming to you, feel free to check our News page on this website so you don't miss a new announcement.

Zima's rebrand has come with many more changes beyond appeal, branding, and formula. As of 2026, Zima has partnered with many recycling facilities and professionals to create a bottle and label that are completely recyclable. With this new model, Zima is committing to reducing our carbon footprint by up to 60%!

Zima's Values and Goals

Zima is proud to be a part of change in the world. Zima partners with farmers who produce their stocks naturally, and our factories are up to date with safety to keep all in our Zima family safe.

In all our long years of product testing, the Zima team has figured out the best ways to ethically make our drinks. If you want to know more, feel free to contact our team using our Contact page!
