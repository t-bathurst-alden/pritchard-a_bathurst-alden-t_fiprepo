Our History


Zima started as a novelty beverage in the early 1990s, and saw its end only years later. Now, with our official rebrand, we aim to make our drink popular once again, with a new variety of flavors, a focus on being a fun and fresh product, and a brand new target demographic.

What's Next?


Our current aim with Zima is to continue to expand our distribution and availability within various retailers and independant sellers, as well as further expanding our already healthy range of flavors! Please be on the lookout for new developments, and if you wish to learn more, please check out our news page!
